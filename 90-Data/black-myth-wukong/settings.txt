The story of Black Myth: Wukong is divided into six chapters, titled "Fire Illuminates the Black Clouds," "Wind Rises at Dusk," "White Dew Born in the Night," "A Winding Path to Purple Mandarin Ducks," "The Sun Sets on the Mortal Dust," and "Unfinished." The game features two endings, and the player's choices and experiences will influence the final outcome.

At the end of each chapter, there are 2D and 3D animated cutscenes that showcase and explore the narrative and thematic elements of Black Myth: Wukong.

The game's setting blends Chinese cultural and natural landmarks. For example, the Dazu Rock Carvings in Chongqing, the Lesser Western Heaven (Xiaoxitian), Nanchan Temple, Iron Buddha Temple, Guangsheng Temple, and the Stork Tower in Shanxi Province all appear in the game. The game also incorporates Buddhist and Taoist philosophical elements.
