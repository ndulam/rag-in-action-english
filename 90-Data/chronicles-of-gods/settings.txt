The story of "Black Myth: Wukong" is divided into six chapters, titled "Fire Illuminates Dark Clouds," "Wind Rises at Dusk," "White Dew at Night," "Purple Mandarin Duck's Melody," "Sunset in the Red Dust," and "Unfinished," and features two endings — the player's choices and experiences will influence the final outcome.
At the end of each chapter, there are 2D and 3D animated cutscenes that showcase and explore the narrative and thematic elements of "Black Myth: Wukong."
The game's setting blends Chinese cultural and natural landmarks. For example, the Dazu Rock Carvings in Chongqing, Xiaoxitian in Shanxi Province, Nanchan Temple, Tiefosi Temple, Guangsheng Temple, and Stork Tower all appear in the game. The game also incorporates philosophical elements from Buddhism and Taoism.
